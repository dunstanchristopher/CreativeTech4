MiMOSA

The Brief: Explore making mixed-reality chairs by using the technique and concept of assembling, a three-dimensional alternative to collage, a technique of composing work by combining various found models (real and physical), and online materials not normally associated with one another. Through this process, we will explore new forms of manufacturing and human factors in design.
